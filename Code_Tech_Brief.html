III. Methodology: Stochastic Intent Calculus (SIC)

This research utilizes the Stochastic Intent Calculus (SIC) framework to manage semantic drift. The engine establishes mathematical friction to ensure output maintains high fidelity to the researcher's original intent, mitigating the "Saturation of Ease" often found in generalist chatbots.

Fidelity & Validation Benchmarks

Validation is achieved through the Fidelity Index ($\Phi$). For AGED 7970, a Veri-CAM threshold of $\tau=0.85$ is required for pedagogical validity ($b=0.38$).
